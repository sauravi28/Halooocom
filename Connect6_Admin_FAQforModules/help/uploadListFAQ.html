• List ID

This is the numerical name of the list, it is not editable after initial submission, must contain only numbers and must be between 2 and 8 characters in length. Must be a number greater than 99.

• List name

This is the description of the list, it must be between 2 and 20 characters in length.

• List Assign To*

Here you select the calling method for this list


Userwise - The data uploaded will be allocated to an individual user.
Predective - The data uploaded will be allocated to a particular campaign
IVR - The data uploaded will be allocated to Voice blast or Robo calling.

• Upload List (csv format)*

Here you'll be uploading the calling data, the format can be downloaded from the Download format option under Upload list.
