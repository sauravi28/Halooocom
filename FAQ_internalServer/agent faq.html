CONNECT 4 Agent Panel FAQ

  1. What is the purpose of this manual?

    This manual serves as a resource for Call Center Managers and Agents to understand how the CONNECT 4 call center solution works and learn how to use its features correctly.

  2. How do I log in as an agent?

    Click on the "Agent" option, then enter your agent login ID, password, and the correct campaign provided by the admin.

  3. What is the InGroup Selection option?

    InGroup Selection allows agents to select which inbound call queue (InGroup) to log into for receiving incoming calls. Admins can also pre-assign InGroups.

  4. What is the Blended Calling option?

    Blended Calling enables agents to handle both inbound and outbound calls simultaneously.

  5. What is the Customer Information section for?

    The Customer Information section allows agents to enter data during live calls. For auto-dialed calls, uploaded lead data is automatically displayed here.

  6. How many custom fields can be added to Customer Information?

    The Customer Information section can be extended up to 40 custom fields based on requirements.

  7. What do the stats display (Inbound, Outbound, Drop, Login Hours)?

    These stats show the count of inbound calls received, total manual/auto-dialed calls, missed calls with caller details, and the agent's total login hours.

  8. How do I make a manual outbound call?

    Click the keypad option in the toolbar, enter the number, and click the dial button.

  9. What are the different call transfer options?

    Transfer options include Local Closer (agent-to-agent), Blind Transfer (external number), Dial with Customer (conference), Leave Con Call (exit conference), and Hung Up Transfer Line (drop conference party).

  10. How do I pause/unpause myself?

    Click the Pause button to temporarily pause yourself for breaks or manual dialing. Click Active to unpause and receive calls.

  11. How do I put a caller on hold?

    Click the Park button to put a live caller on hold, and click it again to retrieve the call.

  12. How do I hang up a call?

    Click the Hang Up button to disconnect a live call, then select a disposition code.

  13. How do I log out of the agent panel?

    Click the Log Out button to exit the agent panel.

  14. Where can I view my scheduled callbacks?

    The Callback button shows a list of callbacks you have scheduled.

  15. Where can I view my call log?

    The Call Log button displays a log of all calls you have made.

  16. What are the transfer modes used for?

    The transfer modes allow you to transfer a live call to another agent within the office, an external number, or initiate a 3-way conference call.

  17. How do I send an internal chat message?

    Use the Internal Chat option to send chat messages to other agents.

  18. How do I view other agents' status?

    Click the Agents View button to see the current status of all logged-in agents.

  19. What does the Live Call indicator show?

    The Live Call indicator shows whether you are currently on a live call (green) or not (red).

  20. Can I make video calls from the agent panel?

    Yes, the toolbar has a Video Call option to make video calls.
